Air Fried Lemon Pepper Wings

Description

This is a simple recipe but relatively healthy. Air Fried wings are the most convenient for our family now. So we just air fry that, throw in a bit of olive oil, and mix with lemon pepper seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken Wings
  • 1/4 Cup Lemon Pepper Seasoning
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Pepper

Steps

  1. Start by washing the chicken wings and then drying them off with a paper towel.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 375 degrees Farenheit.
  3. Sprinkle some salt and pepper onto the chicken wings, then toss them into the air fryer.
  4. Set the air fryer timer to 20 minutes.
  5. Take the chicken wings out, place them in a bowl, add the oil then toss.
  6. Add the lemon pepper seasoning and toss some more. Be generous with the lemon pepper seasoning, as that's where all the flavor comes from! Enjoy
